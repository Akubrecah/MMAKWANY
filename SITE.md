# Mmakwany Guest House — Event Hall Configurator & Quote Generator

## 1. Vision
A "Super App" for hospitality that integrates Accommodation, Event Management, and Dining.
This module focuses on the **Event Hall Configurator & Quote Generator** — allowing guests to browse halls, configure events, and receive instant quotes.
