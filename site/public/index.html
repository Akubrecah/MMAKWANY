A Warm Welcome Awaits You

Nestled in the heart of Kenya, Mmakwany Guest House is your ideal destination for comfort, great food, and memorable events. Whether you're here on business, for a celebration, or simply to unwind — we make you feel at home.

Our dedicated team ensures every guest receives personalized attention, from our freshly prepared Kenyan and continental dishes to our beautifully appointed rooms and our versatile event hall that can host up to 500 guests.
